Discover Top Rafting Routes in the USA

From the wild waters of the West to scenic river canyons, explore our curated rafting adventures across the United States. Perfect for adrenaline junkies and nature enthusiasts.

Colorado River – Grand Canyon, Arizona

Embark on the ultimate whitewater journey through the Grand Canyon. With Class III to V rapids and stunning canyon walls, this multi-day expedition is a bucket-list adventure.

Rogue River – Oregon

Enjoy a mix of exciting rapids and calm stretches in this scenic Pacific Northwest gem. The Rogue River is great for wildlife spotting, including bears and bald eagles.

Gauley River – West Virginia

Known for its intense fall release season, the Gauley River features over 100 rapids including Class V drops. Ideal for advanced rafters craving serious excitement.

Salmon River – Idaho

Float through the "River of No Return" wilderness with a mix of Class III-IV rapids and scenic beauty. This multi-day trip is perfect for groups and families seeking adventure and isolation.
